My Journey

After having followed the academic path of becoming a marketing specialist and after some years of working experience my life changed in an instant. After that I questioned the classic way of living my life and changed my path becoming a yoga teacher (hatha, yin, pre & postnatal). This brought me closed to my purpose and enabled me to go through life following my passion. I am interested in diverse topics so it was just a matter of time that I wanted to learn about coding and front-end development. So here I am now, a yoga teaching markeitng specialist on her journey to becoming an experienced front-end developer.
